## Research Roadmap

1. Chapter 2 focuses on benchmarking genotype-to-phenotype methods and candidate gene prioritization.
2. Chapter 3 consolidates PRS benchmarking, GWASPoker triage, and heritability strategy analyses.
3. Chapter 4 presents integrative EFGPP framework development and tooling synthesis.
4. Chapter 5 covers translational applications for target prioritization and variant interpretation.
5. Appendix A stores additional manuscript-level supporting materials.
